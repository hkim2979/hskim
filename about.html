CORSAIR | SCUF

Corsair is a global developer and manufacturer of high-performance gear and technology for gamers, content creators, and PC enthusiasts. My job as a graphic desinger in this company is to create and come up with ideas for brand campaigns, ad-designs for web/media, and make product designs. I've worked with many content creators and teams that are based on online platform such as NBA2K, Call of Duty, pro-gamers/streamers and etc to come up with the new product designs and campaigns.

About Me

I am a graphic designer and front-end web developer based in Atlanta, Georgia. I have many years of experience working as a graphic designer in gaming tech industry and also working as a freelance/contracted designer at an ad-agency in South Korea as well. I've created, built and solved visual problem - expanding capabilities while also growing business and revenue exponentially. I am best known for saying "let me figure it out" and "I can take care of it" rather than "I can't." And I'm a daily coffee drinker.
